Get Started


Steps to Install

  1. Download 'Canara ai1Pe' from Play Store or App Store.
  2. Allow permissions and verify your mobile number via SMS.
  3. Create a secure 5-digit passcode for login.

Steps to Activate

  1. Link your bank account (Canara or any other bank).
  2. Select activation method: Debit Card, Aadhaar OTP, or Activation Code.
  3. Set your 6-digit UPI PIN to start transacting.
