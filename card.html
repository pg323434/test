Quick Setup & Security Guide


How to Generate a Green PIN
  1. Open the Canara ai1 Mobile App.
  2. Navigate to 'Cards' > 'Green PIN'.
  3. Enter the card expiry date and set your new 4-digit PIN.
  4. Confirm the request using the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
How to Enable Online Transactions
  1. In the Canara ai1 App, go to 'Manage Cards'.
  2. Select 'Domestic' or 'International' usage.
  3. Toggle 'Online / E-Commerce' to ON.
  4. Set your desired daily limit and confirm with your MPIN.
