POS Variants and Saliant Features


MPOS
  • It's a compact and portable device with easy Bluetooth connectivity to smartphones.
  • Suitable for merchants with less card business/delivery agents.
  • Charge slip cannot be printed.
PSTN
  • It's a fixed device with wired network connectivity via landline
  • Suitable for Merchants operating in low network areas i.e. basements.
  • It is non-portable device.
GPRS with/without Printer
  • Tap and Pay facility enabled
  • SIM card and WiFi connectivity
  • Dynamic BHIM-QR facility available.
  • It's a portable device with physical keypad.
  • Charge slip can/cannot be printed.
Android with/without Printer
  • Tap and Pay facility enabled.
  • SIM card and WiFi connectivity
  • Dynamic BHIM-QR facility available
  • It's a portable android touch screen device with on-screen virtual keyboard.
  • Charge slip can/cannot be printed
Hybrid PoS
  • Tap-n-Pay & Card Dip/Insert.
  • Dynamic QR Code.
  • Dynamic BHIM-QR facility available.
  • Inbuilt-PCI PIN keypad for secured transactions.
  • Amex/Sodexo/Diners/International Cards acceptance.
Soft-PoS
  • Tap-n-Pay.
  • Dynamic QR Code.
  • Instant digital Charge-slip via SMS.
  • Cash at PoS.
  • Amex/Sodexo/Diners/International Cards acceptance.
